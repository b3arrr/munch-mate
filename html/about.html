How it works

Swipe what you love! We learn your tastes and serve up personalized recommendations and exclusive offers. Discover everything about your favorite spots and book your table right from the app. Planning a night out with friends? Swipe together and find the perfect place to enjoy!

Our mission

Munch Mate connects consumers with local restaurants and nightlife, making it easier to discover great places to eat and drink. By offering personalized recommendations and group features, we help users quickly find the perfect spot for any occasion. At the same time, we support local businesses by driving new customers their way and promoting exclusive offers, helping them thrive in their communities.
